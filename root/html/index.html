Who Am I?

I am a 30 year old professional who works for a professional consulting company called Matrix Solutions Inc. As a company we specialize in:

  • Assessments, Remediation, & Reclamation
  • Water
  • Spill Response
  • Environmental Permitting & Approvals
  • Environmental Compliance & Monitoring
  • Climate Risk & Resiliance
  • Environmental Management Systems & Audits
  • Data Management & Geomatics

My role within Matrix is as a GIS Developer.

Outside of work I like to play competitive men's league hockey in the fall/winter and golf in the spring/summer. I am also a hard core Edmonton Oilers fan.

I grew up in Olds, Alberta, Canada in a large family with 5 other siblings and my parents. Growing up hockey was a major part of my identity. I was on skates from the ages of 4 and started to gravitate to the elite level of hockey in Atom. I played AA & AAA hockey throughout Atom to Midget and a tiny bit of Junior hockey as well.

When I was 17 years of age I also become obsessed with become a pilot. I entered into ground school at 17 and had achieved my private pilots license just after my 18th birthday. Upon graduation from high school I was accepted into Mount Royal University in their Aviation Diploma program. I quickly learned that flying for me was a huge passion but not one that I wanted to make my day job; flying commercially for some reason took the fun out of flying for me. At that point I decided that flying would be a hobby as mine instead of a career.

When I left Mount Royal, I thought my next calling was going to be aircraft mechanics. I entered into the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology's (SAIT) aircraft maintenance engineers diploma and graduated that a few years later. While working in the industry I realized that that was also something that I did not want to be doing for the rest of my life. After coming to this very similar realization I started to search for a new career and that is when I found out about Geographic Information Systems from my brother who was taking the program at SAIT. That lead me into the career I have today; I could not be happier with that choice.

Career Walk-through

I am a Detail orientated GIS Developer with around 5 years of industry experience. I started out in environmental consulting as a AutoCAD Technician with a company know as ProspEX Resources; after I received my 'AutoCAD Certificate of Completion' from SAIT. At ProspEX I worked to complete technical drawings and field & reporting figures for my company with the AutoCAD design software.

After 2 years of doing this I entered into the 'Applied Bachelors of Geographic Information Systems' at SAIT and as part of my required practicum I gained employment at Matrix Solutions Inc. as a GIS Analyst. While working as a GIS Analyst at Matrix, I created field and reporting figures within the ArcDesktop and ArcPro Software Suites. In addition to creating figures I also performed any spatial and non-spatial analysis needed to supplement the figures I would create for our clients.

While at Matrix, I developed a passion for solving unique and complex problems that we would encounter in our day-to-day work. This lead me down the path of a developer and with Matrix's guidance into a role a permanent role as a GIS Developer. Within this role I have further grown as a professional and built myself up with a multi-faceted skill-set within the python programming language and the ESRI suite of software's including but not limited to:

  • Mapping Automation
  • Extract-Transform-Load (ETL) Design & Development
  • Custom Geoprocessing Tools
  • Enterprise Data Management & Analysis
  • Relational Database Design

Including the aformentioned profession skills I also have a demonstrated ability to work in high pressure environments that require precise time management and fast turn-around's and have developed competent interpersonal and project management abilities.
